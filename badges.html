Badges

Badges are awarded for achievements and milestones on Ampehere. Click any badge on a profile to come here.

Earned Badges
Rich
Awarded to players who have accumulated a substantial amount of coins on Ampehere.
Earn 10,000 coins to unlock this badge automatically.
Automatically earned
Forumer
Awarded to players who have contributed to the Ampehere community through forum posts and discussions.
Get your first forum post or topic approved to unlock this badge.
Automatically earned
Friends
Awarded to social players who have built a large friend network on the platform.
Reach 20 accepted friends to unlock this badge.
Automatically earned
Special Badges
Ampere+
Awarded to players who support Ampehere by purchasing Ampere+ membership.
Purchase Ampere+ from the shop to receive this badge.
Purchased
Admin
Awarded to members of the Ampehere administration and moderation team.
Granted by the platform owner to staff members.
Owner granted
Meowl Badge
A mysterious and rare badge belonging exclusively to the owner of Ampehere. Only one person holds this.
You can't get this. It belongs to the owner.
Owner only
